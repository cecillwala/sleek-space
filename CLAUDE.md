# CLAUDE.md

## Project Overview

Build a modern, premium, responsive company profile website for **SleekSpace Management**, a real estate and property management company.

The website should establish trust, credibility, and professionalism while showcasing the company's expertise in property management and real estate consulting.

This website is NOT a property listings platform.

Do not design the website like BuyRentKenya, Property24, Zillow, or other listing websites.

Instead, position SleekSpace Management as a trusted property management and real estate consulting firm.

# Company Overview

SleekSpace Management is a property management company focused on keeping residential and commercial properties well-run, well-maintained, and comfortable for the people who use them every day.

The company works closely with property owners, developers, landlords, and residents to ensure smooth operations and high standards across every property under management.

Services include service charge management, maintenance coordination, tenant relations, inspections, vendor management, and day-to-day property operations.

SleekSpace Management takes a hands-on approach to property management, helping clients protect long-term property value while creating well-managed environments for tenants and residents.
